Spiced Chickpea Curry

30 minVegetarian

A quick, nourishing vegetarian dinner — ready in 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp cumin; 1 tsp coriander; 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 can chickpeas; 1 can diced tomatoes; 150 ml coconut milk

Steps

  1. Heat oil; cook onion until soft.
  2. Add garlic and ginger.
  3. Add spices, toast briefly.
  4. Add chickpeas and tomatoes; simmer.
  5. Stir in coconut milk and finish.
